About this Module

A weekly objectives planner designed to align team performance and track execution progress. Each team member commits up to 15 goals at the start of every week.

Key Actions

  • Set up to 15 personal goals for the current week.
  • Check off completed goals to update your progress bar.
  • Search and review team commitments by user or week.
  • View the leaderboard to see the top-performing teammates.
