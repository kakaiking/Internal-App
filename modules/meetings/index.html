About this Module

A management hub for logging team syncs, recording agendas, scheduling follow-ups, and tracking post-meeting minutes and action items.

Key Actions

  • Schedule a new standup, retrospective, or team sync.
  • Record detailed agendas, meeting links, and participant lists.
  • Add post-meeting minutes and assign action items.
  • View the Top Organizers leaderboard and search meeting history.
